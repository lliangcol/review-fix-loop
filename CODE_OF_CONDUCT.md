# Code Of Conduct

Review Fix Loop follows the Contributor Covenant principles in a compact form.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Focus criticism on code, behavior, documentation, and tradeoffs.
- Help keep issues and discussions useful for maintainers and users.
- Respect privacy and do not post secrets, private code, or private logs.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or discriminatory language.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Repeated disruption of issues, discussions, or reviews.
- Abusive or bad-faith security reports.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove content; limit participation; or block contributors when behavior harms the project or community.

Report conduct concerns privately through the maintainer contact channels available on GitHub.
